Resumo

Extensão complexa: Additional context about the mindfulness practice session

  • location: string: The physical location where the mindfulness practice took place
  • environment: CodeableConcept: The environmental conditions during the practice session
  • guidance: boolean: Indicates if the session was guided or self-directed

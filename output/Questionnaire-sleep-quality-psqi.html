Questionnaire: Sleep Quality Questionnaire (PSQI-Based)

Official URL: https://2rdoc.pt/ig/ios-lifestyle-medicine/Questionnaire/sleep-quality-psqi Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2025-11-28 Computable Name: SleepQualityPSQI

A validated questionnaire for measuring sleep quality over the past month. Based on the Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index (PSQI) by Buysse et al. Global scores >5 indicate poor sleep quality.

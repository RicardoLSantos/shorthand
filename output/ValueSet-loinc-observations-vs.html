ValueSet: LOINC Observation Codes for Lifestyle Medicine

Official URL: https://2rdoc.pt/ig/ios-lifestyle-medicine/ValueSet/loinc-observations-vs Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2024-11-21 Computable Name: LOINCObservationsVS

LOINC codes relevant for lifestyle medicine observations including HRV, vital signs, anthropometrics, and physical activity metrics. Used as target ValueSet in ConceptMaps for semantic interoperability.

Questionnaire: Physical Activity Questionnaire (IPAQ-Short)

Official URL: https://2rdoc.pt/ig/ios-lifestyle-medicine/Questionnaire/physical-activity-ipaq-short Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2025-11-28 Computable Name: PhysicalActivityIPAQShort

IPAQ-Short Form is a validated 7-item questionnaire measuring physical activity over the past 7 days. Results can be reported as categorical (low, moderate, high activity) or as MET-minutes/week. MET values: Walking=3.3, Moderate PA=4.0, Vigorous PA=8.0. Total MET-min/week = sum of Walking + Moderate + Vigorous MET-min/week.

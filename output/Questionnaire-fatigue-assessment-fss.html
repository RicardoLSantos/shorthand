Questionnaire: Fatigue Assessment Questionnaire (FSS)

Official URL: https://2rdoc.pt/ig/ios-lifestyle-medicine/Questionnaire/fatigue-assessment-fss Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2025-11-28 Computable Name: FatigueAssessmentFSS

The FSS is a 9-item validated questionnaire measuring the impact of fatigue. Each item is rated 1-7 where 1=strongly disagree and 7=strongly agree. The mean of all 9 items yields the FSS score. A score of 4 or higher generally indicates significant fatigue. For comparison: healthy controls typically score around 2.3, while patients with fatigue-related conditions score 4.7 or higher.
